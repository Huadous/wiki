## Mentions in Source
> **Source: [[sources/en_rdma|en_rdma]]**
> - "The second key feature in RdmaEndpoint data transmission is sliding window flow control."
> - "The flow control is to avoid fast transmit side overwhelming slow receive side."
> - "to reduce the overhead of ACKs, the ACK number can be piggybacked in ordinary data message as immediate data."

> **Source: [[sources/en_streaming_rpc|en_streaming_rpc]]**
> - "When the amount of unacknowledged data reaches the limit, the Write operation at the sender will fail with EAGAIN immediately."
> - "At this moment, you should wait for the receiver to consume the data synchronously or asynchronously."

> **Source: [[sources/rdma|rdma]]**
> - "RdmaEndpoint数据传输逻辑的第二个重要特性是滑动窗口流控。"
> - "这一流控机制是为了避免发送端持续在发送，其速度超过了接收端处理的速度。"
> - "RdmaEndpoint内实现了这一流控机制，通过接收端显式回复ACK来确认接收端处理完毕。为了减少ACK本身的开销，让ACK以立即数形式返回，可以被附在数据消息里。"
